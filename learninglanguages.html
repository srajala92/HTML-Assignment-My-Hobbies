SUSANNA RAJALA

Learning another language is like discovering a whole new world of art and connection

Since I was a kid, I have been in love with learning languages. I remember sitting in my childhood bedroom, struggling to read a book of Pablo Neruda's poetry, and promising myself that one day I would be fluent in Spanish. I feel so fortunate that not only have I achieved that goal, but I have also studied basic Russian, French, and Italian. The most amazing part of studying languages has been getting to explore a whole new world of art and music. Learning to speak in other languages has been a truly humbling journey.
