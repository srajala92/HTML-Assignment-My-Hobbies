SUSANNA RAJALA

I love how seeing the world through a camera grounds you in the present

I always say that the two things I can't live without are my bicycle and my camera. Both are so special to me because of the way that they ground me in the present. My favorite thing to do is travel to a new place and wander around, trying to capture the energy of being there with my camera. Taking photos and shooting and editing videos will forever be passions of mine.
