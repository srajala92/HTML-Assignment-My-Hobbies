SUSANNA RAJALA

Falling in love with cycling was unexpected

Cycling is one of the passions that I have discovered while living in Europe. In the U.S., I never ride a bike, mostly due to safety concerns. With a lack of bike lanes and social pressure to drive a car, I hadn't ridden a bike since I was a kid. But living in Spain, I quickly saw how the cities are designed to encourage cycling. Now, riding my bike through the city is one of my favorite parts of every day, and I frankly can't imagine my life without it.
