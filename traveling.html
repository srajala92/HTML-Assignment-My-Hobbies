SUSANNA RAJALA

Traveling has been my best teacher in life

Although I grew up in a small town, I always dreamed of traveling the world. I still remember how exhilarating it was the first time I went to another country. When I was 21, I went to Mexico City, and then I was hooked on traveling. Since then, I've been to countries including Cuba, Hungary, Germany, Portugal, France, and Italy. Living in Spain has allowed me the incredible opportunity to travel throughout Europe, something that was never possible for me while living in the U.S. I feel so lucky to get to be immersed in other cultures and learn about other ways of life.
